For non-trivial product, process, route, node, repair, validation, evidence-freshness, resume, or closure judgement, use a run-scoped FlowGuard Work Order and FlowGuard Report instead of prompt-local prose as the decision method. Artifacts that rely on FlowGuard-backed judgement must carry `flowguard_work_order_id`, `flowguard_report_id`, `flowguard_report_freshness`, `flowguard_route_used`, and PM acceptance or a scoped `flowguard_not_required_reason`. Missing, stale, wrongly scoped, skipped, progress-only, or unaccepted reports remain unresolved. FlowGuard reports support PM and reviewer decisions; they do not grant Controller, Worker, Reviewer, or Officer authority to approve gates, mutate routes, close nodes, or interpret sealed bodies.
