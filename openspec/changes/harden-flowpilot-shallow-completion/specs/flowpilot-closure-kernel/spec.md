## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Closure keeps semantic usefulness separate from lifecycle closure
FlowPilot terminal closure SHALL keep final-user outcome replay separate from
mechanical lifecycle cleanliness and SHALL NOT let clean route, packet, ledger,
or closure-kernel evidence replace user usefulness proof.

#### Scenario: Clean lifecycle evidence does not close shallow output
- **WHEN** lifecycle, route, packet, and ledger reconciliation classify all
  mechanical records as closed or nonblocking
- **AND** the delivered output still matches one or more named current
  shallow-completion traps
- **THEN** PM terminal closure SHALL block completion until PM repairs, mutates,
  explicitly scopes the outcome, or stops for the user.

#### Scenario: Partial completion is user-facing and bounded
- **WHEN** PM cannot eliminate current shallow-completion traps inside the
  available material or authority
- **THEN** PM SHALL label the final output as partial or blocked with the
  missing practical next step named
- **AND** terminal closure SHALL NOT claim full completion for that outcome.
