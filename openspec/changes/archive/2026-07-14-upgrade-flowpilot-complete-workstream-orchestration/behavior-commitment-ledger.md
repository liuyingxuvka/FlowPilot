## Removed surfaces

The dedicated material-intake packet, PM material-understanding memo,
material-sufficiency result/gate/cards, material-specific public facade exports,
replacement-packet repair fields and `packet_reissue` plan kind, and current material test tier have terminal disposition
`forbidden_current_contract`. Their names may remain only in historical
records, explicit forbidden-field lists, and negative tests. No compatibility
translation, missing-field default, or alternate authority is permitted.
